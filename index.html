Present Perfect

Раскройте скобки
1. She _____ (finish) her homework already.
✓ has finished
2. They _____ (visit) London three times.
✓ have visited
3. I _____ (never/see) such a beautiful sunset.
✓ have never seen

Multiple Choice

Выберите правильный вариант
1. Choose the correct form:
A I have went to Paris
B I have gone to Paris
C I have go to Paris

B She has live here for 5 years
C She have lived here for 5 years
